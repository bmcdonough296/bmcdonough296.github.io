Murray City Identity

Identity / Branding / Style Guide

Murray residents are proud of their city and when the centennial was on the horizon Mayor Daniel Snarr proposed a new logo be developed to commemorate the occasion. Working over several months with the city council and then subsequent departments, the logo, department colors, department icons, and the stationery system were created and embraced.
